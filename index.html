We are Inova.

As a business, we are focused on frugal, grassroots innovation. We seek to promote the growth of innovative ideas and businesses. In order to spread our knowledge of innovation and entrepeneurship, we host innovation camps in collaboration with partners like DNB and the Red Cross.
